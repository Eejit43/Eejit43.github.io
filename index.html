Eejit's Tools
Welcome to my site! Here are some handy tools I made whilst learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript!
Time Displays the current time (in your time zone), as well as detailed time information
IP Info Displays your current IP address, and IP provided information
Countdowns Shows various countdowns until major upcoming holidays
Currency Exchange Rates Shows information for various currency conversions
Regex Tools Many useful regex tools, as well as a regex tester
Quick Copy Clipboard display, clear clipboard button, and useful characters
Base64 Encode/Decode Encode and decode to and from Base64 format (binary ASCII)
Base64 Image Encode/Decode Encode and decode images to and from Base64 format
Color Coversions Convert between Hex codes, RGB values, and HSL values, and see those colors displayed
UNIX Time Converter Convert from date strings to UNIX time (in seconds or milliseconds), and back
Case Changer Change a string to uppercase, lowercase, title case, or sentence case
List Sorter Alphabetize, numerize, randomize, and reverse lists that can be defined with custom seperators
Random Number Generator Generate a random number between two numbers
Countdown Timer Simple countdown timer with end time display
Stopwatch Simple stopwatch (displays down to milliseconds)
Counter Press a button to add one to a counter
Roman Numeral Converter Convert to and from roman numerals, with high level thousand supports (bars above numbers)
KeyCode Information Click any key to get the key, key location, key code, char code (ASCII), and char code (unicode).
Minecraft Color Codes Lists all Minecraft color and formatting codes
Tone Indicators List of common tone indicators
Dog Age Calculator Convert between dog and human years (and back!)
Magic Eight Ball Ask a question, and it will predict the future! (but there are more than eight responses!)
Pong Play the classic two player pong game! The left paddle is controlled with [W] and [S], and the right paddle is controlled with the up and down arrows. [esc] can be used to pause.
Snake Play the classic snake game! Alternate between easy, medium, hard, and impossible modes. Use the arrow keys to move, and press the spacebar to pause.
